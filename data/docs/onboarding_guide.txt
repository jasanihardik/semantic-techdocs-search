Title: New Hire Onboarding Guide

Welcome new employees! This guide covers the initial steps to get productive.

First-day checklist:
- Complete HR paperwork.
- Set up your company email and calendar.
- Attend orientation session.
- Get access to internal wikis and documentation portals.

Account setup & passwords:
- Create a strong password on first login.
- Enroll in multi-factor authentication (MFA).
- Read the Password Recovery Process for steps if you lock out of your account.

Training:
- Mandatory security training within first week.
- Product training schedule available in the Learning Portal.

Contacts:
