Title: Email Setup and Configuration

How to configure your company email on desktop and mobile.

Steps:
- On desktop, use Outlook or the web client.
- On mobile, install Outlook app and sign in with corporate credentials and MFA.
- If you forget your email password or need to reset, follow the Password Recovery Process.

Tips:
- Configure email forwarding only with manager approval.
- Use calendar to book meetings; see Onboarding Guide for calendar training.
